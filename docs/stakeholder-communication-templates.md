# Stakeholder Communication Templates

This document provides ready-to-use templates for common stakeholder communications in OctoAcme projects. Consistency in format and tone improves clarity and builds confidence.

## When to Use Each Template

- **Weekly Status Update:** Regular cadence (typically Fridays or Monday morning)
- **Milestone Update:** At the completion of major project phases or milestones
- **Risk Escalation:** When a new high-impact risk is identified
- **Release Announcement:** Before and after a production deployment
- **Incident Communication:** During and after critical production issues

**Subject:** 📢 [Product Name] — Release [Version] — Available [DATE]

**From:** [Product Manager / Release Manager]

---

**Release Summary:**

We're releasing [Product Name] [Version] on [DATE] at [TIME]. This release includes [brief summary of key features].

**What's New:**
- [Feature 1]: [Brief description of customer value]
- [Feature 2]: [Brief description]
- [Improvements]: [Performance improvements, bug fixes, etc.]

**Availability:**
- **Release Date:** [DATE]
- **Release Time:** [TIME] (timezone)
- **Estimated Deployment Duration:** [# minutes]
- **Expected Downtime:** [Yes / No / If yes: duration]

- [Breaking changes, if any]

**Feedback & Support:**

## 5. Incident Communication Template

### Initial Incident Alert (within 15 minutes)

**Subject:** 🚨 [Severity] — [Product/Service Name] — Incident in Progress

**To:** [All relevant stakeholders, support, executives]  
**From:** [Incident Commander]

---

**Incident Summary:**

- **What:** [Brief description of the issue]
- **When:** Started [TIME], detected [TIME]
- **Impact:** [Affected users/features, estimated customer count]
- **Status:** 🔴 Investigating | 🟠 Mitigating | 🟢 Resolved

**What We're Doing:**
- [Current investigation/mitigation steps]
- [ETA for update]

**What to Expect:**
- [Potential customer-facing impact]
- [Workarounds, if any]

**Updates:** We'll send an update every 30 minutes or when status changes.

---

### Status Update (every 30 minutes during incident)

**Subject:** 🔄 [Severity] — [Product/Service Name] — Status Update [#X]

**Status:** 🔴 Investigating | 🟠 Mitigating | 🟢 Resolved

**Progress Since Last Update:**
- [What was investigated/tried]
- [Root cause found / narrowed down]
- [Mitigation in progress]

**ETA to Resolution:** [Estimated time]

---

### Incident Resolution (within 30 minutes of resolution)

**Subject:** ✅ [Product/Service Name] — Incident Resolved — Post-Incident Report

**Incident Summary:**
- **Duration:** [Start time] to [End time] — [Total duration]
- **Impact:** [# affected users, features impacted]
- **Root Cause:** [Brief explanation]
- **Resolution:** [What fixed it]

**What Happened (timeline):**
1. [Time]: [Event]
2. [Time]: [Event]
3. [Time]: [Resolution]

**What We're Doing to Prevent This:**
- [Short-term action]
- [Long-term prevention measure]
- [Owner and due date]

**Blameless Retrospective:**
- A detailed retrospective will be held [DATE] at [TIME]
- [Link to retrospective document]
- All team members invited to share learnings

**Thank You:**
Thank you for your patience during this incident. We're committed to continuous improvement and preventing recurrence.

Questions? [Escalation contact]

## Tips for Effective Communication

1. **Be Timely:** Send updates on schedule; avoid long silences
2. **Be Clear:** Use simple language; avoid jargon
3. **Be Honest:** Admit gaps in knowledge; update as you learn more
4. **Be Data-Driven:** Include metrics, timelines, and measurable impacts
5. **Be Action-Oriented:** Always include what comes next or what you need from stakeholders
6. **Be Consistent:** Use the same format for recurring communications
